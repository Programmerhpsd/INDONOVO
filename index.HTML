WHAT IS INDONOVO TEXTILES?

Indo-Novo Overseas is a brain child of a group of well-experienced Textile professionals who have decided to pool their vast experience and come up with an entity which can cater to the fast-changing requirements of the World Textile industry.

M/S Indo-Novo Textiles Pvt Ltd, a double and multi-fold yarn manufacturing unit which will further help it in enhancing its activity. It has capacity to produce 260 tones of Plied/Soft package( Ready to Dye package) and expecting to enhance its capacity to 400 tones per month With the united experience of the team, the trading venture is expected to grow quite speedily.
